About Me

Hello! I'm Darun A. Mustafa, a web developer based in Stockholm, Sweden.
I specialize in frontend and backend development, creating dynamic and responsive web applications that provide excellent user experiences.

My journey in web development started with a passion for building interactive and engaging websites. Over the years, I have honed my skills in various technologies, allowing me to develop both the visual and functional aspects of web applications.

In my free time, I enjoy listening to music while coding, as it helps me stay focused and inspired. Long walks are another way I clear my mind and think of new ideas. I am committed to continuous learning and often find myself exploring new frameworks and technologies to stay ahead in this ever-evolving field.

Currently, I am preparing to embark on a four-year program in Technology, which I believe will greatly enhance my expertise and open up new opportunities for innovation. I aim to merge my passion for technology with my dedication to learning, striving to create impactful and efficient solutions that make a difference.
